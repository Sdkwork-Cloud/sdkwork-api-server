# v0.1.37 - Commercial S03 Portal Coupon Account Arrival Correlation

- Date: 2026-04-10
- Version: v0.1.37
- Type: patch
- Scope: commercialization `S03` portal reward-history account-arrival convergence

## Delivered

- added additive `account_arrival` to portal `reward-history`, exposing `order_id / account_id / benefit_lot_count / credited_quantity / benefit_lots`
- correlated grant-style coupon redemptions to current workspace commercial-account benefit lots through the real runtime evidence chain `redemption.order_id -> lot.scope_json.order_id`, restricted to `source_type = order`
- synchronized manual portal OpenAPI and shared portal TS contracts with `PortalCouponAccountArrivalSummary` and `PortalCouponAccountArrivalLotItem`
- upgraded portal credits reward-history UI to distinguish:
  - `Arrived to account`
  - `No linked account lot evidence yet`
  - `No account arrival for checkout discount`
