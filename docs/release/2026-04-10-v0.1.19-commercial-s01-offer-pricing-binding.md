# v0.1.19 - Commercial S01 Offer Pricing Binding

- Date: 2026-04-10
- Version: v0.1.19
- Type: patch
- Scope: commercialization `S01` offer-to-pricing convergence

## Delivered

- extended canonical `ProductOffer` with additive pricing binding fields:
  - `pricing_plan_id`
  - `pricing_plan_version`
  - `pricing_rate_id`
  - `pricing_metric_code`
- added deterministic canonical pricing id/metric helpers in `sdkwork-api-app-catalog`
- bound every current portal commercial offer to a canonical pricing plan/rate reference
- exposed offer pricing binding through portal catalog and portal shared TS types
- fixed order creation so `pricing_plan_id` comes from canonical offer pricing truth, not from subscription `target_id`
- kept portal compatibility fields and existing pricing lifecycle surfaces intact
